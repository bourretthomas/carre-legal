Privacy Policy

Domidoku — what we collect (almost nothing) and what we share (only what's needed for ads).

Summary

Domidoku is a single-player puzzle game. We do not run a back-end. We do not have an account system. Your game progress stays on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud, in your own private iCloud storage so it can follow you between your devices — we cannot access it either way. Beyond that, the only data that leaves your device goes to Google AdMob — and only to serve and measure advertisements — unless you have removed ads via in-app purchase.

Read on for the longer version.

1. Who We Are

Domidoku is operated by Carré (“we”, “us”). You can reach us at hello@domidoku.app.

2. What We Store on Your Device & Your iCloud

The following information is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us:

  • Game progress: completed puzzles, best times, current daily streak, ongoing-game saves
  • Settings: sound / music / haptics preferences, your player name, avatar colour, ad-removal status
  • Tutorial state: whether you have seen the tutorial

This data lives in iOS local storage (SwiftData and UserDefaults). If you are signed into iCloud, a small progress summary (completions, best times, streak, hint balance) also syncs through Apple's iCloud key-value storage so your progress follows you between your own devices. That copy lives in your personal iCloud account, is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy terms, and is not visible to us. Everything is wiped when you uninstall the App or tap “Reset all progress” in Settings; the mid-game save never leaves the device it was played on.

3. Advertising (when ads are enabled)

Unless you have purchased the in-app ad-removal entitlement, the App displays banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or access:

  • Your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant tracking permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Coarse, non-precise location derived from your IP address
  • Information about the ads you see and interact with (impressions, clicks, completions of rewarded ads)
  • Standard device characteristics (model, iOS version, language)

AdMob uses this data to deliver, measure, and (if you have consented) personalise advertising. Google’s own privacy policy applies to this processing: policies.google.com/privacy

In the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent before requesting personalised ads. You can review or change that consent at any time via Settings → “Ad privacy options”.

4. In-App Purchases

Purchases (for example, “Remove Ads” or hint packs) are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. Apple shares only a transaction receipt with the App so we can grant the entitlement on your device. We do not see, store, or process your name, email, payment card, billing address, or any other purchase detail. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, use Settings → “Restore purchases”.

5. Tracking Permission

On first launch (after a brief delay), iOS will ask whether the App may track you across other companies’ apps and websites. This is a system prompt controlled by Apple, not by us. Your answer is honoured by Apple at the operating-system level — we cannot override it.

  • If you allow tracking, AdMob may use your IDFA to deliver personalised ads.
  • If you deny tracking, ads still appear (unless removed via IAP), but they are non-personalised.

You can change this answer any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Domidoku.

6. Children

Domidoku is rated 4+ but is not specifically directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at hello@domidoku.app and we will assist where we are able.

7. Data Retention

Because we do not operate a back-end, we do not retain any data ourselves. Data stored locally on your device persists until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data processed by Google AdMob is retained according to Google’s own retention policies, linked above.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, the EU under GDPR, the United Kingdom under UK GDPR, or California under CCPA / CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent or opt out of certain processing.

Because we do not hold any of your personal data ourselves, most of these rights are exercised directly:

  • Local data: tap “Reset all progress” in Settings, or uninstall the App.
  • Ad personalisation: revoke tracking consent via iOS Settings, and/or use the in-app “Ad privacy options” to revisit your UMP consent choice.
  • Apple purchase data: contact Apple Support.
  • AdMob processing: see Google’s own privacy controls.

If you have a specific request or believe your rights have not been respected, please contact us at hello@domidoku.app.

9. International Users

The App is available worldwide via Apple’s App Store. By using the App, you understand that your device-level data may be processed by Google AdMob in the United States and other countries where Google operates. Apple processes purchases and identifiers in accordance with its own published policies.

10. Security

Because no personal data leaves your device through us, the primary security boundary is iOS itself, which protects the App’s sandbox using standard platform mechanisms. Apple’s App Tracking Transparency and StoreKit frameworks provide additional safeguards for identifier access and payments. We do not store passwords, payment data, or sensitive personal information at any point.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” below will reflect the latest revision. Material changes will be highlighted via in-app release notes. Continued use of the App after a change means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints about this Privacy Policy? Reach out to hello@domidoku.app. We will respond as quickly as we reasonably can.
